Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, cream butter and sugars until fluffy. Add vanilla and eggs.
  4. Mix in dry ingredients, then fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet and bake for 9-11 minutes.
  6. Cool and enjoy!

Creamy Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. In a pan, cook pancetta until crispy. Add garlic and cook briefly.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs and Parmesan.
  4. Drain pasta, reserving some water. Toss with pancetta off heat.
  5. Add egg mixture quickly, stirring to create a creamy sauce. Add pasta water if needed.
  6. Season with pepper and serve with extra cheese.
