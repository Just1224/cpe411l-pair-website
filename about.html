About Recipe Haven

Recipe Haven is a passion project created by Justine and Emerhald, dedicated to sharing simple, delicious recipes for home cooks of all levels. Whether you're baking cookies or whipping up pasta, we aim to make cooking fun and accessible. Founded in 2023 as part of CPE 411L - Activity 2, our site features user-friendly instructions and high-quality ingredients lists. We believe in the joy of homemade meals and are always experimenting with new flavors!

Feel free to contact us for recipe suggestions, collaborations, or feedback. We'd love to hear from you!
